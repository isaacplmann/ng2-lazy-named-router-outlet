Lazy Aux Outlet

Routing a lazy loaded module into a named router outlet orphans the primary outlet content.

  1. Click "Lazy Aux Outlet"
  2. Click any page link (except for your current page)

Results: "page1 works!" will never be replaced. Repeating the process will continue adding orphaned pages to the DOM.

Note: "Sync Aux Outlet" does not cause this problem.
